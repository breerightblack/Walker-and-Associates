Featured Case · 2024

Isaac Hayes Estate
vs. Trump Campaign

When the Isaac Hayes estate discovered that "Theme from Shaft" was being played without authorization at Trump campaign rallies, they called Attorney James L. Walker, Jr. Walker filed in federal court — and won. A federal judge ruled that the Trump campaign must stop using the iconic song, establishing an important precedent for artists' rights nationwide.

  • Federal injunction granted against Trump campaign
  • Estate's copyright fully upheld in Southern District ruling
  • Case covered by CNN, MSNBC, Fox News, and national press
  • Named a landmark win for artists' rights
