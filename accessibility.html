Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 2026

Walker & Associates is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all visitors to our website, including people with disabilities. We continually work to improve the accessibility and usability of this site for everyone.

Our Standard

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, as a guiding standard for this website's design and content. This is an ongoing effort, and we periodically review the site to identify and address accessibility issues.

Feedback

If you encounter a barrier while using this website, or need assistance accessing any content or feature, please let us know so we can address it. Contact us at info@walkerandassoc.com or (770) 847-7363, and we'll do our best to provide the information or service you need through an alternative method while we work on a fix.
