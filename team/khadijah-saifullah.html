Khadijah Saifullah is the Director of Finance for J. Walker and Associates, LLP, responsible for overseeing the firm's financial operations, budgeting, compliance, and reporting. With a background in financial management and a deep commitment to organizational excellence, Saifullah ensures the firm's financial health supports its mission and growth.

In her role, Saifullah manages accounts receivable and payable, client billing, payroll, and financial reporting. She works closely with firm leadership to develop and monitor budgets and ensure compliance with all applicable financial regulations and professional responsibility rules.

Saifullah brings a values-driven approach to financial management, believing that a well-run financial operation is the backbone of a firm that can consistently deliver exceptional legal services.
